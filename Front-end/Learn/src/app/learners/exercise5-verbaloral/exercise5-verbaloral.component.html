Look at the following sentences to understand why we need to use Past perfect.

i) The man went to Lake Malawi. After, he went to the airport.

ii) The man went to Lake Malawi after he went to the airport.

In sentence i) which event is first? Going to Lake Malawi or the airport?

In sentence i) which event is first? Going to Lake Malawi or the airport?

It’s very difficult to hear any difference! That is why past perfect is important – it tells us when things happen in the past. Past perfect makes communication easier! It also makes our sentences more complex.
