Look at the following sentences to understand why we need to use Past perfect.

i) The man went to Lake Malawi. After, he went to the airport.

ii) The man went to Lake Malawi after he went to the airport.

In sentence i) which event is first? Going to Lake Malawi or the airport?

Answer: Going to Lake Malawi

In sentence i) which event is first? Going to Lake Malawi or the airport?

Answer: the airport

Look at the following sentence:

“When he finished his swim he realized he had forgotten to bring a change of clothes”

Write the past simple

Answer: he finished his swim he realized

Write the past perfect

Answer: he had forgotten to bring a change of clothes

Which event happened first? Finishing his swim or forgetting his clothes?

Answer: forgetting his clothes

How do we write past perfect?

Answer: Past Perfect = Subject +had + past participle of verb
